📱 How to install Miau on your phone

Miau is a progressive web app (PWA) — it installs directly from the browser, no App Store or Google Play needed.

🍎 On iPhone (Safari)

⚠️ Safari is required. You can't install the app to the home screen from Chrome or another browser on iPhone.
  1. Open Safari and go to app.miauapp.ro
  2. Tap the Share icon — the square with an arrow pointing up, in the bottom bar
  3. Scroll down the list of options
  4. Tap Add to Home Screen
  5. Optionally rename it → tap Add (top right)

The app appears on the home screen like any other app. It opens in full screen, without the address bar.

💡 Don't see "Add to Home Screen"? Make sure you've finished onboarding (the first setup screen) before trying to install.

🤖 On Android (Chrome)

  1. Open Chrome and go to app.miauapp.ro
  2. Tap the 3 dots (menu) in the top right corner
  3. Tap Add to Home screen (or Install app)
  4. Confirm → tap Add

The app appears on the home screen.

❓ Frequently asked questions

Does it work offline too?
Yes — after the first open, the app is saved on your phone and works fully offline. Data is stored locally, on your device.

How do I uninstall it?
Like any app: press and hold the icon on the home screen → Remove or Uninstall.

⚠️ Uninstalling also deletes local data. Do an Export JSON from Settings first, so you don't lose your history.

Can I use the app on multiple phones?
Yes, but data doesn't sync automatically. Use the Export / Import JSON feature in Settings to transfer data from one phone to another.
