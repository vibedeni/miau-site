In the Guides section you'll find how to set up email reports and how to back up your data.

What Miau does

⏱️

Step-by-step timer

Guides the daily treatment with timers for each step — antihistamine, Staloral, waiting period.

💧

Automatic supply tracking

Automatically deducts drops and pills with each treatment. Warns you when supplies are running low.

📋

Protocol set by you

Enter the protocol prescribed by your doctor, with initiation and maintenance phases. The app knows today's dose.

📅

History and symptoms

Log symptoms after each treatment. Chart showing the trend over the last 14 days.

📧

Email reports

Optional: receive an email after each treatment with the dose, symptoms, and remaining supplies.

🔒

Data stays on your phone

No server, no account. Data is stored locally, on your device.

⚠️ Important note: Miau is an organization tool, not a medical device. It does not replace your doctor's prescription and does not provide medical advice. Always follow your allergist's instructions.

Why isn't it in the App Store?
Miau is a PWA (Progressive Web App) — it installs directly from the browser on your phone, following a few simple steps. No App Store or Google Play needed, no account, no registration step.
Is my child's data safe?
Data is stored only locally, on the phone or browser running the app — and only there. Nothing is transmitted anywhere, there's no database, no one has access to it — not even us.
Does it work offline?
Yes. The browser allocates a special cache for the last downloaded version, so the app can be used even when the phone isn't connected to the internet.
Why is it free, and will it stay free?
There's no server behind the app. Email notifications are set up by the user via EmailJS — free, with no involvement from us. No server = no costs = always free.

Miau was born out of the need to support a complex protocol while always keeping an eye on supplies. No more counting minutes, no more counting days — the app does it automatically for us. It makes life easier and, above all, makes immunotherapy possible even when the child isn't with the parent. 🐾
